5 Of The World Fastest Sports Car


  • Bugatti Chiron Supersport 300++:: This car holds the title of the fastest production car in the world, with a top speed of 304mph. It's powered by an 8.0-liter, quad-turbocharged engine producing 1,600hp.

  • Rimac Nevera: This electric supercar can go from 0-60mph in just 1.9 seconds and has a top speed of 258mph. It's powered by a 1,914hp electric motor.

  • SSC Tuatara: This car has a twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing up to 1,750hp and has a top speed of 295mph.

  • Bugatti Mistral: This roadster has a claimed top speed of 261mph with the roof off and is powered by a quad-turbo W-16 engine.

  • Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut: This car has a theoretical top speed of over 310mph, but it's yet to be achieved in real-world testing.
